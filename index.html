About Me

Starting out in 2015 on an old computer with 4 cores and 4GB of Memory playing Garry's Mod I quickly found myself in love with the game, I had played for about 2 thousand hours by 2017 then my Steam account was hacked and I had lost everything, soon after I had gotten a new one and resumed working on my first community, the community was in development for about 2 years (I was working on it in my past time, as a beginner knowing nothing), and then it had finally released, I was so happy when that had happened, then everything came crumbling down when I could no longer afford server payments and I had to shut it down. I've since dabbled in a few communities working my way through games such as Rust, Minecraft & FiveM until I found Perimeter Gaming in 2021, a community full of people based around FiveM, I was quickly on-boarded to help with my expertise in development having done it for so long and here I am today, still working on that community and a Minecraft Network I co-own with a good friend of mine. Thanks for reading and I hope that you enjoyed my story -- I know it's not much but I don't have very much to share.
